Background

Real estate agents shouldn't
carry a key for every property.

Aliseda manages thousands of properties across Spain. Their agents — each handling a portfolio of multiple properties — had to carry physical keys for every one of them. Scheduling viewings, coordinating access, and managing incidents required manual coordination that slowed everything down.

Aliseda's solution was to install smart locks across its properties. The design challenge was building the app that would make those locks actually useful — giving agents digital access, incident management, and portfolio visibility in a single tool.

"A minimum viable product designed in 10 days that covers all the needs of the client — from digital property access to incident management and portfolio control."

The need

An app that allows real estate agents to open and close properties digitally, report problems to the technical team, and monitor nearby properties in their available portfolio.

The solution

A focused MVP covering all three needs — delivered as a high-fidelity prototype in 10 days, validated against the development team's technical requirements.

Incident control panel

A real-time dashboard
for incident management.

The home screen gives agents an immediate global view of all incidents — categorised by status (unresolved, pending, open, unassigned) and type (missing keys, water alerts, lock failures, occupancy warnings). Real-time charts show volume by week and severity distribution.
